How It Works

For Investors

  1. Create an account and complete verification
  2. Choose your investment amount and term
  3. Transfer funds using your preferred payment method
  4. Track your investments and returns through your dashboard
  5. Receive regular repayments with interest

For Borrowers

  1. Fill out the loan application form
  2. Upload required documents (Aadhar Card, PAN Card, Medical Certificate)
  3. Our team reviews your application
  4. Upon approval, receive funds directly in your account
  5. Repay the loan in easy installments
